How to Set Up IPTV on Firestick in Canada

The Amazon Firestick is the most popular device for IPTV in Canada. Here's how to set it up:

  1. Enable "Apps from Unknown Sources" in Firestick Settings > My Fire TV > Developer Options
  2. Install the Downloader app from Amazon App Store
  3. Download IPTV Smarters Pro or TiviMate app
  4. Enter your flixtele login credentials (provided after subscription)
  5. Start watching 25,000+ channels instantly!
