What is 4K Ultra HD?

4K Ultra HD (also known as UHD) offers four times the resolution of standard 1080p HD. With 3840 x 2160 pixels, you get:

  • Sharper, more detailed images
  • Better color depth and contrast
  • HDR (High Dynamic Range) support
  • Cinema-quality viewing experience
