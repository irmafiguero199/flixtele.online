IPTV on Smart TV Canada

Setup guide for Samsung, LG, Sony, and other Smart TVs

Quick Setup

To setup IPTV on Smart TV: 1) Download Smart IPTV app from your TV's app store, 2) Note your MAC address, 3) Visit siptv.app and enter your details, 4) Restart the app. Or use IPTV Smarters if available.

Samsung Smart TV (Tizen)

  1. Press Smart Hub button on remote
  2. Go to Apps
  3. Search for Smart IPTV or IPTV Smarters
  4. Click Install
  5. Open the app and note your MAC address
  6. Visit siptv.app on your phone/computer
  7. Enter your MAC address and M3U URL (provided by flixtele)
  8. Click Submit
  9. Restart the app on your TV
  10. Start watching!

LG Smart TV (webOS)

  1. Press Home button on remote
  2. Open LG Content Store
  3. Search for Smart IPTV
  4. Click Install
  5. Launch the app and note your MAC address
  6. Visit siptv.app on another device
  7. Enter MAC address and M3U playlist URL
  8. Restart app on TV

Sony Android TV

  1. Open Google Play Store
  2. Search for IPTV Smarters Pro
  3. Click Install
  4. Open the app
  5. Select Login with Xtream Codes API
  6. Enter your flixtele credentials
  7. Click Add User
  8. Wait for channels to load

Alternative: Use External Device

If your Smart TV doesn't support IPTV apps, use an external device:

  • Amazon Firestick: Best option, affordable, easy to use
  • Android TV Box: Powerful, full Android experience
  • Apple TV: For Apple ecosystem users
  • Chromecast: Cast from your phone

Troubleshooting

Some TV brands restrict IPTV apps. Try searching for "Smart IPTV" or use an external device like Firestick instead.

Double-check your MAC address and M3U URL. Make sure there are no typos. Try restarting the app and your TV. If still not working, contact our support.

Use ethernet connection instead of WiFi if possible. Close other apps running on your TV. Check your internet speed (need 25+ Mbps for HD).
