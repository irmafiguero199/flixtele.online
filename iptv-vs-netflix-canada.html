IPTV vs Netflix: What's the Difference?

IPTV offers live TV channels (like cable) with 25,000+ channels including sports, news, and live events. Netflix offers on-demand content (movies and series) with no live TV. Many Canadians use both services together.

Why Use Both?

Many Canadians subscribe to both IPTV and Netflix because they serve different purposes:

  • IPTV for: Live sports, news, events, and channel surfing
  • Netflix for: Original series, documentaries, and exclusive movies

Combined cost: ~$24-36/month vs $120+ for cable - still huge savings!
